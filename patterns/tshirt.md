# Pattern: T-Shirt (sleeveless body + short sleeve edging)

## Notes
- This shirt is designed to fit the project torso sizes in `patterns/body.md`.
- Best workflow: dress the body before final head attachment.
- Keep tension even; fabric should be snug but not stretched.

## Standard T-shirt (2.5 mm, DK)
### Body tube

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | ch 24, join (do not twist), sc around | 24 |
| 2-4 | sc around | 24 |
| 5 | sc 8, ch 3, sk 3, sc 10, ch 3, sk 3 | 24 |
| 6-7 | sc around (work into chain spaces) | 24 |
| 8 | (sc 10, dec) x2 | 22 |
| 9 | sc around | 22 |
| 10 | (sc 9, dec) x2 | 20 |

Fasten off.

### Sleeves (work into each armhole opening)
Each armhole has 6 stitches (3 skipped + 3 chains).

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | Join yarn, sc around armhole | 6 |
| 2 | (sc 2, dec) x2 | 4 |

Fasten off and weave ends inside.

## Keychain mini T-shirt (2.0 mm)
### Body tube

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | ch 18, join, sc around | 18 |
| 2-3 | sc around | 18 |
| 4 | (sc 4, ch 2, sk 2, sc 6, ch 2, sk 2, sc 2) | 18 |
| 5-6 | sc around | 18 |
| 7 | (sc 7, dec) x2 | 16 |

Fasten off.

### Mini sleeves
Each mini armhole has 4 stitches (2 skipped + 2 chains).

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | Join yarn, sc around armhole | 4 |

Fasten off.

## Fit check
- Standard shirt should sit over the torso 24-st widest area and taper at upper chest.
- If your gauge is loose, add one extra plain round before the first decrease round.
