# Pattern: Backpack (body + flap + straps)

## Notes
- Backpack is worked as a small pouch, then sewn to the back or left removable with straps.
- Keep stitch tension firm so the bag holds shape.
- Optionally add a tiny button bead under the flap.

## Standard backpack (2.5 mm, DK)
### Bag body (worked in rounds)

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | ch 7, sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc 4, 3sc in last ch, sc 4 on opposite side, inc in last | 14 |
| 2 | inc, sc 4, (inc) x3, sc 4, (inc) x2 | 20 |
| 3 | BLO sc around (forms base edge) | 20 |
| 4-9 | sc around | 20 |

Fasten off body.

### Flap (worked in rows)
Attach yarn to back-center stitch of top edge. Work across 8 stitches.

| Row | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | sc 8 | 8 |
| 2 | ch 1, turn, sc 8 | 8 |
| 3 | ch 1, turn, dec, sc 4, dec | 6 |
| 4 | ch 1, turn, sc 6 | 6 |
| 5 | ch 1, turn, dec, sc 2, dec | 4 |

Fasten off.

### Straps (make 2)
- ch 18
- Starting in 2nd ch from hook: sl st 17
- Sew ends to top and bottom back corners of the backpack.

## Keychain mini backpack (2.0 mm)
### Bag body

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | ch 5, sc 3, 3sc in last ch, sc 2 opposite side, inc in last | 10 |
| 2 | inc, sc 2, (inc) x3, sc 2, (inc) x2 | 16 |
| 3 | BLO sc around | 16 |
| 4-7 | sc around | 16 |

### Mini flap
Work across 6 stitches at top back edge.

| Row | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | sc 6 | 6 |
| 2 | ch 1, turn, sc 6 | 6 |
| 3 | ch 1, turn, dec, sc 2, dec | 4 |

### Mini straps (make 2)
- ch 12
- sl st 11 back down the chain

## Assembly and placement
1. Center backpack on upper back, below neck join.
2. Tack top corners first, then bottom corners.
3. Add straps over shoulders and stitch where they meet front torso.

Placement target:
- Standard: backpack height about 6 rounds tall.
- Mini: backpack height about 4 rounds tall.
