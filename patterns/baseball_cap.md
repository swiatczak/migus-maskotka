# Pattern: Baseball Cap (Standard + Keychain)

See also: [Baseball Cap Cup — 5 Vertical Color Sectors](cap/cup-5-sectors.md)

## Yarn & tools
- DK cotton, hook 2.5 mm (Standard) / 2.0 mm (Keychain)
- Stitch marker (continuous rounds)

## Crown (worked in continuous rounds)
### Standard size (2.5 mm)

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | MR 6 | 6 |
| 2 | inc x6 | 12 |
| 3 | (sc, inc) x6 | 18 |
| 4 | (2sc, inc) x6 | 24 |
| 5 | (3sc, inc) x6 | 30 |
| 6–9 | sc around | 30 |
| 10 | sl st around (optional for crisp edge) | 30 |

Fasten off with a long tail for sewing brim.

### Keychain mini (2.0 mm) – quick table

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | MR 6 | 6 |
| 2 | inc x6 | 12 |
| 3 | (sc, inc) x6 | 18 |
| 4 | (2sc, inc) x6 | 24 |
| 5–7 | sc around | 24 |
| 8 | sl st around (optional) | 24 |

## Brim (worked in rows, turned)
### Standard brim (matches 30-st crown)
Work in **rows** and keep stitches tight.

| Row | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | ch 7, sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc 5 | 6 |
| 2 | ch 1, turn, inc, sc 4, inc | 8 |
| 3 | ch 1, turn, inc, sc 6, inc | 10 |

Fasten off, leave a long tail.

### Keychain mini brim
Option A (2 rows):
- Row 1: ch 6, sc 5 (5)
- Row 2: inc, sc 3, inc (7)

Option B (3 rows):
- Row 1: ch 6, sc 5 (5)
- Row 2: inc, sc 3, inc (7)
- Row 3: inc, sc 5, inc (9)

## Assembly (brim placement)
1. Find the **front center** of the crown.
2. Align brim’s top edge to crown edge, centered.
3. Sew brim with whip stitch through both loops of brim and front loops of crown for a neat join.

### Placement guide
- Standard: sew brim across **10 sts** centered on front.
- Keychain: sew brim across **7–9 sts** depending on brim choice.
