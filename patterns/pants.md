# Pattern: Pants (Standard + Keychain)

## Notes
- Legs are worked in continuous rounds from the cuff up.
- Use invisible decreases for smooth shaping.
- Stuffing: pants are **not stuffed**; keep fabric firm.

## Standard (2.5 mm, DK)
### Leg (make 2)
| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | MR 6 | 6 |
| 2 | inc x6 | 12 |
| 3–8 | sc around | 12 |
| 9 | (sc 4, dec) x2 | 10 |
| 10–11 | sc around | 10 |

Fasten off first leg. Do **not** fasten off second leg; continue to join.

### Join + Body of pants
Join legs with legs facing same direction.

| Rnd | Instruction | Sts |
|---:|---|---:|
| 12 | sc around leg 2 (10), ch 2, sc around leg 1 (10), ch 2 | 24 |
| 13 | sc around (include chains as stitches) | 24 |
| 14–16 | sc around | 24 |
| 17 | (sc 10, dec) x2 | 22 |
| 18–19 | sc around | 22 |
| 20 | (sc 9, dec) x2 | 20 |
| 21–22 | sc around | 20 |

Fasten off. Optional: add drawstring with surface slip stitches.

## Keychain mini
Use scaling factor ~0.75–0.85 of Standard counts depending on your gauge.
